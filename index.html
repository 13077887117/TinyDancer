Tinydancer

An tiny android library for displaying FPS & percentage of time with two or more frames dropped

View on GitHub Download .zip Download .tar.gz

Why we made it

Perf Matters, but how do you measure it? We took the techniques/practices from this talk (given by Facebook at this past DroidConNYC), and created an overlay that shows you the FPS of your app in real time.
